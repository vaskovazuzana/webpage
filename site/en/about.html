About me

About me

I am a structural designer specializing in reconstructions, project documentation, and BIM modeling. I work in a field where technical precision meets design thinking.

I am drawn to reconstructions because of the work with what already exists — understanding the structure, context, and constraints before proposing something new.

I see BIM as a methodology, not just software. The digital model allows me to verify solutions, coordinate trades, and work with higher accuracy.

In my work, I value precision, clarity, and practicality. Good documentation is not a formality — it is the foundation for a quality result on the construction site.
